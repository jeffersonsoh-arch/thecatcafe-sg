House rules
Before you arrive
To keep our cats happy, healthy, and stress-free — and our guests safe.
1
Minimum age 4 years old. Children under 12 must be accompanied and strictly supervised by an adult.
2
Hand sanitisation is required before entering the cat area. Our staff will assist you.
3
Handle cats gently. Do not carry, lift, or chase them without staff assistance.
4
No outside food or drinks permitted in the cat area.
5
Arrive on time — slots are released to walk-in guests after a 10-minute grace period.

Every cat here was once a stray. We believe in Adopt, Don't Shop — and hope that spending time with our rescued cats inspires more people to open their homes to ex-strays. Ask our staff about supporting local shelters.
